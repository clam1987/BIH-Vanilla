About Us

In 2014, Blend It Healthy was born out of a personal desire to find nutritious and immune-boosting products. It has since expanded its mission to educate, inspire, and cultivate a community of health-conscious individuals dedicated to embracing food as preventive medicine and a source of vitality.


An authentic, family-oriented business that cares about the well-being of their customers, Blend It Healthy aims to serve the best, nutritious beverages -- free from additives and full of vitality.


“Together with our customers, we create a community in which we strive for wellness, consolidate knowledge, and celebrate life through wholesome nutrition.” - Emily & Albert

Our Story

The lives of Emily and Albert took an unexpected turn in 2009 when Emily was diagnosed with breast cancer. The shock of the news prompted Albert to research on how best to manage his wife's health. It became very apparent that nutrition is of the utmost importance. In his research, he found the best nutrition comes from fruits and vegetables. This knowledge in spired them to share their vision of health and wellness.

So in 2014, was born. Since then, we have cultivated a community of passionate individuals - encouraging them on their health journeys as they embrace food as a preventive medicine.
