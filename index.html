Keeping You Informed on Our Response to Coronavirus (COVID-19)

We hope you, your families and loved ones are staying safe and healthy during this difficult time.
We want to thank you for your trust and for allowing us to continue to serve you.

At Blend It Healthy, you can expect:

  • Requiring our staff to wear face masks and gloves.
  • Reinforcement of proper handwashing, hygiene, and health standards.
  • Increased frequency of cleaning all high touch point areas as well as hand washing to every 30 minutes.
  • Requiring our staff to remain at home if they are not feeling well.
  • Continuing to offer curbside pickup and delivery.
  • Following guidance with any additional recommendations made by the CDC or World Health Organization.
